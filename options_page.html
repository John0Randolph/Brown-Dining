Tips & Common Mistakes

- Ensure that all your foods are separated by commas. Example: "blueberry pancakes, pie, chicken and waffles, donut".

- Ensure that your spelling is correct

- Ensure that you have WIFI and that dining.brown.edu isn't down when refreshing.

- You may put parts of food names, so "pancakes" will pick up "blueberry pancakes", "chololate chip pancakes", etc

- It may be necessary to hit the refresh button multiple times

- If you're having a bug or have feedback, please hit the bug/feedback button below!
